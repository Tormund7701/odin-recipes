Philly Cheese Steaks

What is Philly Cheese Steak?

Thinly sliced steak cooked on a griddle, with onions, peppers, and finally the top player, the cheese sauce. Traditionally served on a sandwich roll or grinder.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh rib-eye steak
  • 1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons of Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
  • 4 Sandwich rolls
  • 1 (8 fl oz) can of cheese whiz

Steps

  1. Add oil to a large non-stick skillet.

  2. Saute the onions and 2 tablespooons of our teriyaki sauce until the onions are soft

  3. From here, we add our sliced meat and cook until the meat browns. Add the remaining tablespoon of teriyaki sauce and stir together.

  4. Add or spray the cheese onto the meat & onions and stir till its melty and gooey.

  5. Remove from skillet and place it onto your sandwich roll. Enjoy!
