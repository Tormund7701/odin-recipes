Chicken Kiev

What is Chicken Kiev?

Chicken Kiev is a you guessed it Chicken dish where the chicken has been hollowed out and filled with butter thats been supercharged with garlic and parsley.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 Tablespoons of parsley
  • 6 Tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 8 oz Skinless, boneless, chicken breasts, halved and pounded to 1/4th inch thickness
  • 1 Cup of flour
  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • 2 Cups of panko bread crumbs
  • 1 Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 2 Cups of vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Grind garlic, salt, and parsley together and mix well using a mortar and pestle. Add butter into the mixture and pound until well combined, refrigerate after.

  2. Season your chicken breast with salt and pepper, then place the butter mixture in the center of the chicken breast. Fold the breast over the butter and form it into a round ball. Wrap the chicken ball in plastic wrap and place it into the freezer for 30 minutes.

  3. Whisk flour and 2 teaspoons of salt together in one bowl. Whisks eggs together in a second bowl and pour panko bread crumbs into a third bowl.

  4. Remove the chicken orbs from the plastic wrap and lightly coat them in flour before dipping into the beaten eggs and finally add them to the bread crumbs. Return to plastic wrap and re-freeze for 15 minutes.

  5. Heal oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.

  6. Remove breaded chicken orbs from plastic wrap, and gently place them in the hot oil for about 1 minute per side. After, transfer chicken to the baking sheet and sprinkle with salt and cayenne pepper.

  7. Bake in a pre-heated oven for 15 to 17 minutes. You will hear the butter start to sizzle on the baking sheet when done An inserted thermometer should read 165 degrees. Let sit for a few minutes, and serve. Enjoy!
