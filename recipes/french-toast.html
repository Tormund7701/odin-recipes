French Toast

What is French Toast?

French toast is the result of drowning bread in an egg custard then frying the bread up on a hot buttered skillet.

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Starting with your wet ingredients, whisk together milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt till you have an egg custard.

  2. Next we're going to heat up the skillet, and add butter to it

  3. From here, we dunk our bread into the custard, and let it soak on both sides till thoroughly drenched.

  4. Add the custardy bread to the skillet, and let cook on one side, 3 to 4 minutes. We're aiming for a golden brown color, so 3 to 4 minutes per side is usually enough.

  5. Remove from skillet and plate up with some butter and syrup. Enjoy!
